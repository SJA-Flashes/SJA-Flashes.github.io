2018 Robot

Our 2018 robot focused on the switch, portals, and vault. Pneumatics were used to pinch power cubes in the front and to push power cubes into the switches out from the top. One member of the drive team would go to a portal so the robot could be fed power cubes on the top, and release them into the switches. We could also put power cubes in the vault to get power ups. If the opportunity came, we could climb onto another robot and be lifted up to get an extra ranking point. We successfully did a triple climb with the help of team 4592.
