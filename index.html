About Us

From St. Augustine, Florida, the Flashes! Since our founding in 2015, we've competed in the FIRST Robotics Competition. We give our flashes the opportunity to jump start their future in STEM through valuable mentoring and FIRST.

2019 Robot

Our 2019 robot was able to deliver both balls and hatch panels to the cargo ship, and to the 1st level of the rocket. Compliant wheels mounted on an elevator allowed us to pick up balls from the ground and deliver them. On the back of our robot, an arm controlled by a pneumatic cylinder allowed us to carry hatch panels from the loading station to delivery. To learn more about our robots click here.

What is FIRST?

FIRST is a nonprofit organization that promotes student interest in STEM fields. There are 4 FIRST programs for different grade levels: FIRST LEGO League Jr., FIRST LEGO League, FIRST Tech Challenge, and the FIRST Robotics Competition You can access the FIRST website here.
