About

Located in St. Augustine, Florida, The Flashes! We are team 5753 of the FIRST Robotics Competition as team 5753. Our rookie year was in 2015 at the Orlando Regional. Although we are a small team, we can still come together to build amazing robots. We finished our rookie year off by winning the Rookie Inspiration Award. We are committed to letting every student express their robot building skills in a fun environment.

2018 Robot

Our robot this year focused on the switch, portals, and vault. Pneumatics were used to pinch power cubes in the front and to push power cubes into the switches out from the top. One member of the drive team would go to a portal so the robot could be fed power cubes on the top, and release them into the switches. If the opportunity came, we could climb onto another robot and be lifted up to get an extra ranking point. We successfully did a triple climb with the help of team 4592.
