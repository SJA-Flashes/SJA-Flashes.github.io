About

Located in St. Augustine, Florida, The Flashes! We are team 5753 of the FIRST Robotics Competition. Our rookie year was in 2015 at the Orlando Regional. Although we are a small team, we can still come together to build amazing robots. We finished our rookie year off by winning the Rookie Inspiration Award. We are committed to letting every student express their robot building skills in a fun environment.

2018 Robot

Our robot this year focused on the switch, portals, and vault. Pneumatics were used to pinch power cubes in the front and to push power cubes into the switches out from the top. One member of the drive team would go to a portal so the robot could be fed power cubes on the top, and release them into the switches. We could also put power cubes in the vault to get power ups. If the opportunity came, we could climb onto another robot and be lifted up to get an extra ranking point. We successfully did a triple climb with the help of team 4592. View our other robots here.

What is FIRST?

The mission of FIRST® is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.
